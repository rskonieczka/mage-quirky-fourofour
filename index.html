Quirky FourOFour Page for Magento

Simple module for adding a meta refresh to 404 pages that will redirect to a search results page made from the 404 pages url. E.G. http://yoursite.com/something/random will be redirected to http://yoursite.com/catalogsearch/result/?q=something+random
